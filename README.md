🚖 OLA Bookings & Rides Analysis

An end-to-end data analytics project analyzing OLA cab booking patterns, ride performance, cancellations, customer behavior, and revenue — built using **MySQL**, **Power BI**, and a real-world scale dataset.


📌 Project Overview

This project dives into OLA's ride booking data to uncover operational insights that can help improve driver allocation, reduce cancellations, and optimize revenue. The analysis spans the full data workflow — from raw CSV ingestion and SQL-based querying to an interactive Power BI dashboard.

🎯 Business Questions Answered

- What is the overall ride success rate across all bookings?
- Which vehicle types cover the most distance on average?
- How many rides are cancelled by customers vs. drivers — and why?
- Who are the top 5 most loyal customers by ride count?
- What is the revenue generated from successfully completed rides?
- How do driver and customer ratings vary across vehicle types?
- What are the most common reasons for incomplete rides?
- What share of payments are made via UPI?

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **MySQL** | Data querying, view creation, aggregation |
| **Power BI Desktop** | Interactive dashboard & visualization |
| **CSV** | Raw data source |

💡 Key Insights

1. Ride Success Rate: A significant portion of bookings result in successful rides; cancellation analysis reveals customer-side cancellations outnumber driver-side ones.
2. Vehicle Preferences: Prime Sedan shows distinct distance and rating patterns compared to other vehicle types.
3. Top Customers: A small group of repeat customers account for a disproportionate share of total rides.
4. Revenue Leakage: Incomplete rides represent lost revenue; identifying their reasons helps prioritize fixes.
5. Payment Trends: UPI is a widely used payment method, signaling strong digital payment adoption among users.
